Are you a business? We like you too!!

How it works for you Customers.

Step 1

Do what you already do. Eat at your favorite restaurants. Go to your favorite stores. Visit your favorite places and do your favorite things.

Step 2

Look for the Punchr logo. Ask if they use Punchr. Demand they use Punchr for their Loyalty Cards. You are a loyal customer and you deserve it.

Step 3

Grab your phone and text that business' Punchr code to our shortcode.

Step 4

You will get a text message back within seconds that will confirm your punch and update you on your Punchr Count.

How it works for you Business.

Step 1

Reward your customers for the loyal patronage of your business. Loyal customers are what keep us going and sometimes they deserve a little kickback.

Step 2

All of your customers punches will be tracked by us. You don't have to worry about making cards, printing cards, employee security, etc.

Step 3

Track and configure account from any computer with internet access. Change your specials. Configure your security settings.

Step 4

Check out our reporting and leader boards. See how many of your customers are punching their Punchr loyalty cards, how often, and when. While you are at it you could even make a few suggestions of what kind of reporting you would like to see.
